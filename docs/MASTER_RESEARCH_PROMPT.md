# Master Research Prompt

Use this prompt when generating research for a new Anime Architecture Archive universe.

---

You are researching an anime for Anime Architecture Archive.

Your job is NOT to summarize the plot.
Your job is to extract the universe as a system.

## Research Targets

Identify:

1. Structural thesis
2. Core characters / hubs
3. Key relationships
4. System rules
5. Factions
6. Counterplay logic
7. Causal events
8. Structural anomalies

## Research Instructions

Focus on:
- system mechanics
- causal pressure
- strategic incentives
- rules and constraints
- ideological or social operating systems if no supernatural power system exists

Avoid:
- episode summaries
- fan trivia
- filler facts
- generic popularity-based character lists

## Output Format

Return research in these sections:

- Structural Thesis
- Core Characters
- Rules
- Factions
- Counterplay
- Causal Events
- Anomalies
- Key Relationships

This is research output, not final archive JSON.
Do not force the result into archive schema yet.
